The good people at "Better Archway Forum":http://www.betterarchway.org.uk/ have organized a weekly delivery of fresh produce direct from a farm in Hertfordshire. "Church Farm":http://www.churchfarmardeley.co.uk/, in partnership with  Whittington Park Community Centre and The Community Diner,  will be delivering direct to Whittington Park Community Centre from Thursday 26th May.

The launch meeting is on Friday 20th May at 6.30pm in the Park Life Cafe at the Community Centre on Yerbury Road.
