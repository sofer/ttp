The steering group meet once a month in different peoples houses in Tufnell Park.

The group is made up of representatives from different project groups such as growing or energhy and of people who want to be more involved with the organisation of Transition Tufnell Park

Steering group meetings happen on the first Monday of the month.

If you are interested in joining the steering group please come to green drinks (Every last monday) and talk to us about it
