      <h1><span style="font-size: 26pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">What is Permaculture?</span></h1> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Permaculture can be best described as a collaborative (wiki-style) project in which people around the world collectively design solutions to the problems of building a sustainable future. Its theory underpins the ideas behind Transition Tufnell Park.</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Rob Hopkins, the founder of the Transition Towns concept, was a Permaculture teacher for 10 years before coming up with his idea of a community-wide response to the issues surrounding Peak-Oil &amp; Climate Change. </span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Permaculture has been around since the late 70s, and has since spread from Australia all around the world. It always has a local flavour, as it draws from indigenous knowledge and vernacular styles as well as from current science and modern design. </span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Permaculture’s strength lies in its strong ethical foundations- Earth Care, People care, Fair Shares- and its many Principles including: collaboration over competition, thoughtful action rather than reaction, valuing the marginal and designing from patterns to details - ideas that people across the globe can use simply wherever they are, and in whatever they’re doing.</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Permaculture is a grassroots movement growing every year, come be part of the solution and Be the change you want to see in the world. We are the ones we’ve been waiting for.</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p><b style=""><span style="font-size: 18pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Courses:</span></b><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;"></span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Introduction to Permaculture – 2 days (usually a weekend)</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Permaculture Design Certificate - 72 hours (usually over 2 weeks or 5 weekends)</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p> <h3><span style="font-size: 18pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Links:</span></h3> <h2><b><span style="font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Permaculture in London <a name="_Hlt279511206"><span style="">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></a><a href="http://www.londonpermaculture.com/"><span style="">www.LondonPermaculture.com</span></a></span><span style="font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;"><a href="http://www.londonpermaculture.com/"><span style=""></span></a><span style=""></span></span></b></h2> <p><b><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></b></p> <p><b><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Tahoma,sans-serif;">Naturewise (N.W.
