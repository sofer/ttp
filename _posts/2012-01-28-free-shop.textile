De-clutter after Christmas, recycle resources and shop till you drop all for free!

Come and help us create a new way to exchange, 11am-3pm, at the market outside the Tufnell Park Tavern

Please donate items to help create this free shop.

Drop off for donations will be:

11am-3pm at the market the week before, Saturday 21st January and between 10.00am to 12pm on the 28th.

We are looking particularly for toys, kids and adults clothes, books, CDs and records, and other things in good condition.

No broken things or electrical goods please.
