Bring your bike for a check up and advice on to how to fix it. You'll work on your own bike with help from a mechanic. Free, but donations welcome.

When?
On 5th November and on the first Monday of every month, 7-9 pm

Where?
Kentish Town Health Centre, 2 Bartholomew Road
