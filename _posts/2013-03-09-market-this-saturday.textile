Bike day at the food market

Your weekly local food market will be hosting a bicycle fiesta this coming Saturday - there will be a Dr Bike stall, bicycle-powered music and a family-friendly bike tour with Tufnell Park Cycles Safely to School for the younger bike enthusiasts. Transition Free Press will also be available alongside the usual delicious food fare.

*When?*
11am-3pm, Saturday 9th March (bike tour at 2pm; market is every Saturday)

*Where?*
In front of the Tufnell Park Tavern, 162 Tufnell Park Road, N7 0EE
